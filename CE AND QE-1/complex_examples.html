🎮 LEVEL 1 BOSS: The Gatekeeper of Imagiana

Challenge: Find magnitude and argument of z = 3 − 4i.

✅ Solution:
|z| = √(3² + (−4)²) = 5
arg(z) = tan⁻¹(−4/3) = −53.13° (4th quadrant)

⚔️ LEVEL 2 BOSS: The Twin Shadows (Operations Arena)

Given: z₁ = 2 + 3i, z₂ = 1 − 2i. Find sum, difference, product, quotient.

✅ Solution:
z₁ + z₂ = 3 + i
z₁ − z₂ = 1 + 5i
z₁·z₂ = (2+3i)(1−2i) = 8 − i
z₁ / z₂ = (2+3i)/(1−2i) = (−4 + 7i)/5

🧭 LEVEL 3 BOSS: The Polar Guardian

Challenge: Convert z = 1 − i to polar form.

✅ Solution:
r = √2, θ = −45° → z = √2(cos(−45°) + i sin(−45°))

⚡ LEVEL 4 BOSS: The De Moivre Dragon

Challenge: Compute (1 + i)^6 using De Moivre’s theorem.

✅ Solution:
1 + i → r = √2, θ = 45° → (1 + i)^6 = (√2)^6 (cos(6×45°) + i sin(6×45°)) = 8(cos270° + i sin270°) = −8i

🌙 LEVEL 5 BOSS: The Circle Keeper (Roots of Unity)

Challenge: Find all cube roots of unity (z^3 = 1).

✅ Solution:
1, −1/2 + i(√3/2), −1/2 − i(√3/2)
