Example Problems: Root Relations

Practice using Vieta's formulas!

‚≠ê Example 1: Sum and Product

Q: For the equation $2x^2 + 5x - 3 = 0$, find the sum ($\alpha + \beta$) and product ($\alpha \beta$) of the roots without solving.

‚úÖ Solution:
Identify coefficients: $a=2, b=5, c=-3$.
Sum of roots: $\alpha + \beta = -\frac{b}{a} = -\frac{5}{2} = \mathbf{-2.5}$
Product of roots: $\alpha \beta = \frac{c}{a} = \frac{-3}{2} = \mathbf{-1.5}$

‚≠ê Example 2: Finding Value from Roots

Q: If $\alpha$ and $\beta$ are the roots of $x^2 - 4x + 7 = 0$, find the value of $\frac{1}{\alpha} + \frac{1}{\beta}$.

‚úÖ Solution:
From Vieta's formulas: $\alpha + \beta = -(-4)/1 = 4$ and $\alpha \beta = 7/1 = 7$.
Combine the expression using a common denominator: $\frac{1}{\alpha} + \frac{1}{\beta} = \frac{\beta + \alpha}{\alpha \beta}$.
Substitute the sum and product: $\frac{4}{7}$. The value is $\mathbf{4/7}$.

‚≠ê Example 3: Forming Equation from Roots

Q: Form a quadratic equation whose roots are 3 and -5.

‚úÖ Solution:
Sum of roots = $3 + (-5) = -2$.
Product of roots = $3 \times (-5) = -15$.
Use the form $x^2 - (\text{Sum})x + (\text{Product}) = 0$.
$x^2 - (-2)x + (-15) = 0$
$\mathbf{x^2 + 2x - 15 = 0}$ (Note: Any non-zero multiple, like $2x^2 + 4x - 30 = 0$, is also correct).
