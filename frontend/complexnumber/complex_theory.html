🎯 Complex Number Operations & Polar Form

Welcome, cadet! Let’s level up your complex-number game — from rectangular to polar and beyond.

🧩 Level 1 – The Portal to the Complex Realm

A complex number is written as z = a + ib where a is the real part and b is the imaginary part.

Think: a = x-coordinate, b = y-coordinate — together they form a point on the Argand plane.

Checkpoint Task: Plot z = 3 − 4i → point (3, −4)

⚔️ Level 2 – Basic Combat Training — Operations

Addition: (a+ib)+(c+id) = (a+c)+i(b+d) — vector addition on the plane.

Subtraction: (a+ib)−(c+id) = (a−c)+i(b−d).

Multiplication: (a+ib)(c+id) = (ac−bd)+i(ad+bc) — geometric meaning: rotation + scaling.

Division: (a+ib)/(c+id) = (a+ib)(c−id)/(c²+d²) — multiply numerator & denominator by conjugate.

Power move: multiplying by i rotates a point 90° counterclockwise.

🧭 Level 3 – The Polar Conversion Portal

Rectangular: z = a + ib. Polar: z = r(cosθ + i sinθ) = r e^{iθ}.

Modulus: r = √(a² + b²). Argument: θ = tan⁻¹(b/a) (choose correct quadrant).

Mini Quest: Convert z = 1 + i → r = √2, θ = 45°, so z = √2(cos45° + i sin45°) = √2 e^{iπ/4}.

⚡ Level 4 – Power-Ups: Multiplication & Division in Polar Form

Multiplication: multiply magnitudes, add angles.
Division: divide magnitudes, subtract angles.

Use De Moivre’s theorem for powers: (r e^{iθ})^n = r^n e^{inθ}.

Mini Quest: z₁ = 2(cos30° + i sin30°), z₂ = 3(cos45° + i sin45°) → z₁z₂ = 6(cos75° + i sin75°).

🧩 Level 5 – Roots & Master Spells

n-th roots: r^{1/n} [cos( (θ + 2kπ)/n ) + i sin( (θ + 2kπ)/n )], k = 0..n−1.

Mini Quest: Cube roots of 8(cos0° + i sin0°) are 2(cos0°+i sin0°), 2(cos120°+i sin120°), 2(cos240°+i sin240°).
