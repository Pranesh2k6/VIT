⚡ Energy & Power – The Flow of Work

Ah, Cadet! You’ve mastered work — now learn how energy and power fuel every action!

🔋 Level 1 – What is Energy?

Energy is the capacity to do work. It exists in different forms: mechanical, heat, electrical, etc.

Mechanical Energy = Potential Energy + Kinetic Energy

🏞️ Level 2 – Potential Energy

The energy stored due to an object’s position or configuration.

Formula: U = mgh

Higher the object, greater the energy reserve — like charging a cosmic battery!

🚀 Level 3 – Kinetic Energy

The energy possessed by a moving object.

Formula: KE = ½mv²

Speed is the secret weapon, Cadet! Double the speed → four times the energy!

⚙️ Level 4 – Work-Energy Theorem

The net work done on an object equals the change in its kinetic energy.

W_net = ΔKE

⚡ Level 5 – Power

Power measures how fast work or energy is done or transferred.

P = W / t = (F × v)

Think of it as how quickly you can “spend” your energy XP!
