🎮 LEVEL 1 – Kinetic Energy

Q1. Find the kinetic energy of a 2 kg ball moving at 3 m/s.

✅ Solution:
KE = ½mv² = ½ × 2 × 3² = 9 J

🏞️ LEVEL 2 – Potential Energy

Q2. A 5 kg object is raised to a height of 4 m. Find its potential energy.

✅ Solution:
U = mgh = 5 × 9.8 × 4 = 196 J

⚔️ LEVEL 3 – Work-Energy Theorem

Q3. A body’s velocity increases from 4 m/s to 6 m/s. Its mass is 3 kg. Find the net work done.

✅ Solution:
ΔKE = ½m(v₂² − v₁²) = ½ × 3 × (36 − 16) = 30 J

⚙️ LEVEL 4 – Power Output

Q4. A motor does 600 J of work in 20 s. Find its power.

✅ Solution:
P = W/t = 600 / 20 = 30 W

🏆 LEVEL 5 – Mixed Challenge

Q5. A 10 kg object falls from 5 m. Find the speed on reaching the ground and check energy conservation.

✅ Solution:
U = mgh = 10 × 9.8 × 5 = 490 J
KE = ½mv² = 490 ⇒ v = √(98) = 9.9 m/s
✅ Energy conserved!
