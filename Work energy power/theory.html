🎯 Work in Physics – The Hidden Energy Currency

Ah, Cadet! Ready to unlock the secrets of *Work*? Let’s power up your physics XP!

🧩 Level 1 – Unlocking the Concept

Work is done only when a force causes displacement in the direction of the force.

Formula: W = F × d × cosθ

Push a wall all you want — no movement, no XP! Work = 0 J if displacement is zero.

⚔️ Level 2 – Direction Dilemma

If θ = 0° → Maximum work done
If θ = 90° → No work
If θ = 180° → Negative work

Remember, cadet! Friction is your enemy — always doing negative work 😈

🧗 Level 3 – Types of Work
