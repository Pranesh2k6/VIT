🎮 LEVEL 1 – Basic Work Calculation

Q1. A person pushes a box with a constant force of 30 N through a distance of 4 m along a smooth floor.
Find the work done.

✅ Solution:
𝑊 = 𝐹 × 𝑑 × cos(0°) = 30 × 4 × 1 = 120 J

⚔️ LEVEL 2 – Angle Between Force and Displacement

Q2. A force of 50 N acts at an angle of 60° to the direction of motion and moves an object 5 m.
Find the work done.

✅ Solution:
𝑊 = 𝐹 × 𝑑 × cos(60°) = 50 × 5 × 0.5 = 125 J

🧗 LEVEL 3 – Positive, Negative, and Zero Work

Q3. A student drags a 10 kg bag (force = 40 N) 3 m across the floor while friction applies 15 N opposite to motion.
Find:
a) Work done by the student
b) Work done by friction
c) Net work

✅ Solution:
a) Wₛ = 40 × 3 = 120 J
b) Wf = 15 × 3 × cos(180°) = −45 J
c) Wₙₑₜ = 120 − 45 = 75 J

🧙‍♂️ LEVEL 4 – Variable Force (Graph Type)

Q4. Force increases linearly from 0 N at x = 0 m to 20 N at x = 4 m. Find the work done.

✅ Solution:
W = ½ × base × height = ½ × 4 × 20 = 40 J

🏆 LEVEL 5 – Mixed Concept (Angle + Opposing Force)

Q5. A man pulls a 25 kg cart with a 60 N force at 30° above the horizontal for 6 m. The frictional force is 20 N.
Find the net work done.

✅ Solution:
W₁ = 60 × 6 × cos(30°) = 311.76 J
W₂ = 20 × 6 × cos(180°) = −120 J
Net work = 311.76 − 120 = 191.76 J
