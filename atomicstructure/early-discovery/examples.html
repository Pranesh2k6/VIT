⭐ Example 1: Subatomic Particles Count

Q: An ion has 17 protons, 18 neutrons, and 18 electrons. What is the ion's charge and mass number?

✅ Solution:
Charge = Protons - Electrons = 17 - 18 = -1
Mass Number = Protons + Neutrons = 17 + 18 = 35

⭐ Example 2: Rutherford's Findings

Q: What key conclusion did Rutherford draw from the observation that most alpha particles passed straight through the gold foil?

✅ Solution:
He concluded that the atom is mostly empty space.
